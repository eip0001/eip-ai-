faq
what does "funding the agent" mean?
EIP.AI is designed to be fully autonomous — meaning it needs to fund its own operations. Subscription revenue directly pays for compute, API costs, and the agent's on-chain transaction budget. The goal is for the agent to become self-sustaining through a mix of subscriptions and protocol fees.
can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel at end of the current period. Annual plans can be cancelled with a prorated refund for unused months.
what is the agent builder?
A chat-based interface where you describe your agent's purpose, strategy, and parameters. At the end, you get a working agent.js file and .env config you can deploy anywhere — Railway, Render, or your own server.
what chain does the agent run on?
Base (Ethereum L2) by default. Ultra plan agents can be configured for Ethereum mainnet, Arbitrum, Optimism, or Polygon.
why is pricing below market?
We're in early access. Founding members get launch pricing locked in forever. As the platform matures, prices will rise — but your plan never changes.
